About Dr. Parikshit N. Mahalle

Dr. Parikshit Narendra Mahalle is working as Professor at Department of Computer Engineering, at Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology, Pune Since 1st July, 2021. Dr. Parikshit N. Mahalle has obtained his B.E degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, Amravati, India and M.E. degree in Computer Engineering from Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune, India. He completed his Ph. D in Computer Science and Engineering specialization in Wireless Communication from Aalborg University, Aalborg, Denmark. He has more than 19 years of teaching and research experience.
